Summary[edit]

Gagana Samoa: Fu‘a o Sāmoa
English: Flag of Samoa
Deutsch: Flagge Samoas
Español: Bandera de Samoa
Māori: Pīwari o Hāmoa
Slovenščina: Zastava Samoe

Drawn using construction details at [1].

The five stars represent the moonlight and the date back to the 9th century which means blue=water and hope red=land and blood. The blue color represent the liberty and the health.
